These files are used to create the exogenous specification of Canadian net trade by BA and either the h17 timeslice or the entire 8760 domain.

The trade data were sent by NARIS team and represent PLEXOS results by the main interconnections on the North American continent.

These are disaggregated by means of sharing out imports and exports by a region in its interconnection using ReEDS reference data for Canadian exchange.

The values are scaled to annual levels by ReEDS reference data rates of changes relative to 2024 (the source year for the trade dataset).
