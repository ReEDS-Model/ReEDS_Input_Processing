- EIA-930 Load Zones: This shapefile represents the areas for which hourly load data are reported in EIA Form 930 (https://www.eia.gov/electricity/gridmonitor/about). Load is reported for each balancing authority (BA) region, and for BAs that are RTOs, load is also reported for each of the BA's subregions (sub-BAs). BA boundaries are taken from the Balancing Authorities shapefile (with modifications to the Public Service Company of New Mexico and Western Area Power Administration Desert Southwest Region boundaries), and sub-BA boundaries are taken from the RTO Regions shapefile (with modifications to the Southwest Power Pool and PJM sub-BA boundaries). For BAs with subregional load data (aside from Public Service Company of New Mexico), only the sub-BA boundaries are represented in this shapefile.
