1 RGGI allowance equals the authorization of a regulated fossil fuel power plant 25 MW and greater to emit 1 short ton of CO2.

Past and current RGGI allowances can be downloaded from https://www.rggi.org/allowance-tracking/allowance-distribution.
The sum for the column 'CO2 Allowance or Base Budget' was used (total number of CO2 allowances allocated by each state).

Future RGGI allowances (regional scale) through 2030 were provided by Cooper Tamayo, cooper.tamayo@rggi.org.

The RGGI allowances are found in rggicon.csv (units of metric tons) and participating states in rggi_states.csv.
